# F6: Contacts CRM

**Purpose:** Recruiter database.

## In scope
- Auto-populated from F3 extraction pipeline.
- Fields: name, company, email, LinkedIn URL, phone (if found), jobs they posted, notes.
- Dedup by email or LinkedIn URL.
- Manual add/edit.
- Export to CSV.
- **Tags:** `in-house` / `agency`, `responsive` / `ghosted` / `not-contacted`, plus custom tags.

## Out of scope (v1)
- Email automation.
- LinkedIn messaging integration.
